Beyond the Office

Hobbies & Interests

Surfing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, computers, cooking and chess — different ways to train discipline, creativity and strategic thinking.

🏄 Surfing

Surfing keeps me grounded. Reading the ocean, waiting for the right set and adapting to changing conditions is a practical exercise in patience and awareness.

Growing up near the Amalfi Coast gave me an early love for the Mediterranean, and wherever I travel, I look for waves.

🥋 Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

BJJ is a constant reminder that leverage beats brute force. The mat rewards consistency, humility and technical precision.

Staying calm under pressure translates directly into how I approach risk, incidents and difficult decisions at work.

💻 Computers & Tech

Technology is a genuine passion. I enjoy understanding how systems work, writing code and automating repetitive processes.

CS50 has been a practical way to strengthen my technical foundation and connect governance work with data, systems and automation.

SQL Python HTML / CSS / JS Automation GRC Platforms

👨‍🍳 Cooking

Cooking is a ritual for me. Growing up in Southern Italy, I developed a love for simple dishes done well: pasta, fresh seafood and family recipes.

Cooking is also a small project every time: planning, timing, sequencing and attention to detail make the difference.

♟️ Chess

Chess teaches you to think ahead, evaluate risk, recognise patterns and adapt when conditions change.

I enjoy playing online during breaks and studying classic openings. Every game is a compact strategy problem.
